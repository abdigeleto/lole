Latest News

A Joyful Baptism Sunday

We rejoice and give glory to God for His wondrous work in the hearts of His people! It was with great joy that our church family witnessed the baptism of nine members. This beautiful ordinance is a powerful, public declaration of their faith and new life in Jesus Christ.

Each testimony was a testament to the saving grace of God, and we pray that they will continue to walk in a manner worthy of the gospel. Please join us in celebrating and encouraging these brothers and sisters in their journey of faith.
